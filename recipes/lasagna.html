Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

All instructions were created with the use of A.I. ensure each step is correct before attempting.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat; drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  7. Add half of the ground beef, followed by a layer of marinara sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Top with the final layer of noodles, remaining marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
