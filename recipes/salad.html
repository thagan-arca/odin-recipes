Salad

Description

Salad is a versatile dish that can be made with a variety of fresh vegetables, fruits, nuts, and dressings. It's a healthy option that can be served as a side or a main course.

All instructions were created with the use of A.I. ensure each step is correct before attempting.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (e.g., lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup nuts (e.g., walnuts, almonds, or pecans)
  • 1/4 cup salad dressing (e.g., vinaigrette, ranch, or balsamic)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, shredded carrots, feta cheese, olives, and nuts.
  2. Drizzle the salad dressing over the top and toss gently to combine.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use.
